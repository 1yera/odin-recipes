Chorizo Street Tacos

Burrito, a common cylindrical food item of Mexican and American origin consisting of a tortilla wrapped around a mixed filling of such ingredients as meat, cheese, beans, and vegetables. The burrito differs from the taco in that the circular tortilla in the latter case is simply folded, not wrapped.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10-inch) flour tortilla
  • 3/4 cup cubed, cooked ribeye steak, warmed
  • 1/4 cup Mexican rice, heated
  • 1/4 cup pinto beans, heated
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon diced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Fold a paper towel around tortilla. Microwave on High until pliable, 5 to 10 seconds.
  2. Lay tortilla flat on a plate. Layer steak, rice, beans, cheese, onion, and cilantro in a line across the middle of the tortilla. Fold opposing edges of the tortilla to overlap the filling. Roll 1 of the opposing edges around the filling and tuck tortilla to form a burrito.
